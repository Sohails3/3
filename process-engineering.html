Process Engineering Industrial Placement

During my year in industry I worked on a production line

  • Focused on improving the efficiency of a production line by developing innovative solutions to address challenges in its mechanical operation. Working with cross-functional teams, improving analytical, technical, communication, teamwork and problem-solving skills.
  • Significantly enhanced the performance of machines, through analysis and optimising adjustments. Reducing downtime of one machine from 5.09% to 2.28% within one month, leading to an additional 216,000 bottles produced and saving 315kg of plastic waste, through reducing damaged bottles.
  • Standardised procedures, through creating documentation and training procedures out to operators.
  • Advancing waste reduction initiatives, including reducing the water used in wash cycles.
  • Designing and Manufacturing projects, such as a reject system for incorrectly orientated bottles.
  • Daily Data Analysis on Microsoft Excel to constantly identify which areas needed improvement and develop insights that informed key decisions on production improvements.
  • Found and implemented optimal parameters of the machinery for the packaging of a new product.
  • Developed a digital application where production data can be recorded on Microsoft Power Apps.
  • Created a Web scraping script using Python, that would automatically extract various data.
